Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?
We offer a comprehensive range of services including logo design, flex printing, and installation services. Our team specializes in creating custom designs, high-quality printing solutions, and professional installation services for both indoor and outdoor applications.
How long does it take to complete a project?
Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. Typically, logo designs can take 3-5 business days, while printing and installation projects usually take 2-3 days. We always provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.
What materials do you use for printing?
We use high-quality, weather-resistant materials for all our printing projects. Our materials include UV-resistant vinyl, eco-solvent inks, and premium flex materials that ensure durability and vibrant colors for both indoor and outdoor applications.
Do you provide installation services?
Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our printing projects. Our experienced team ensures safe and secure mounting, proper alignment, and quality assurance for every installation.
What is your pricing structure?
Our pricing is based on the scope of work, materials used, and project requirements. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs. Contact us for a free consultation and quote.
Do you offer design revisions?
Yes, we include multiple design revisions in our packages to ensure you get exactly what you want. We work closely with you throughout the design process to achieve your vision.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve Zirakpur and surrounding areas. However, we can accommodate projects in nearby regions. Contact us to discuss your specific location requirements.
How can I get a quote?
You can get a quote by contacting us through our website, phone, or email. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your requirements and provide a detailed quote.
